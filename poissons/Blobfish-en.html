Blobfish

Geographic area: Off the Australian and Tasmanian coasts
Depth: Between 600 and 1,200
Food: It mainly eats sea pens, sea snails or crabs. It can also feed on marine snow
Life expectancy: They can live up to 130 years
Family: Psychrolutidae
Height: varies from 30 to 65 centimeters
