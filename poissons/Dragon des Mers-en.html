Sea Dragon

Geographic area: tropical areas
Depth: between 1 and 15 meters
Diet: small crustaceans such as tubifex, brine shrimp, cyclops, or daphnia
Life expectancy: 50 years
Family: Pomacentridae
Size: 14 cm maximum
