Mammifères Amphibiens Oiseaux Poissons Reptiles

Jellyfish

Geographic area: North Sea, in the Atlantic and in the English Channel
Depth: the surface water layer of the littoral zone
Food: mainly herring larvae and small crustaceans
Life expectancy: from a few days to two months for small species, one to two years for larger ones and some species can be immortal
Family: Coelenterates
Size: From a few cm to 2 m in diameter
