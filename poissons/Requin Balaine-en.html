Mammifères Amphibiens Oiseaux Poissons Reptiles

Whale Shark

Geographic area: tropical and temperate to warm waters of the Atlantic, Pacific and Indian Oceans
Depth: occurs between the surface and 120 m deep but it can also dive to a depth of 1000 m
Diet: feeds only on small prey such as plankton and krill, but also on algae, small crustaceans, small squid or fish under 10 cm
Life expectancy: 100 years
Family: Rhincodontidae
Size: 5.5 to 10m
